The Origin Rift

How a glitch in reality became a movement

In 2022, our founder — a relic hunter known only as "The Archivist" — stumbled upon a warehouse filled with prototypes from defunct space programs, forgotten tech labs, and avant-garde ateliers. Instead of seeing junk, they saw echoes of unrealized tomorrows. That discovery sparked an idea: what if we could curate the most evocative objects from timelines that never fully materialized?

Thus, fab-garmrent was born. We began as a small collective of 5 creators, reverse-engineering the aesthetics of the future-past. Today, we've grown into an interdimensional guild of 30+ artisans, engineers, and storytellers, each dedicated to one mission: to make the impossible wearable, collectible, and tangible.

Every drop is a limited window into another version of reality — a chance to own a piece of a future that could have been. Welcome to the VERSE.
